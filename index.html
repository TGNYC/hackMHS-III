What is hackMHS?

hackMHS is Millburn High School's hackathon. The first hackMHS was in October of 2015, and the second was in May of 2016. A hackathon is a programming competition in which prizes are given, but there are also lots of fun side-activities! Everything is free, including food, shirts, and more.

Who can come?

All high school students are invited! You don't have to have programming experience or have gone to a hackathon before. If you're in college or older, we would love it if you could register as a mentor.

When is it?

It's on Saturday, December 3rd, from 9am to 9pm. We will post a full schedule soon.

Never been ?

You can let hackMHS III by your first! hackMHS III is designed with first time hackers in mind, wich many mentors and workshops to help you!

How much does it cost?

Nothing at all! hackMHS is completely free to all participants. While you're here, you'll get free food and free stuff!

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop, phone, chargers, and excitement about new things! There will be specifics posted on the Facebook page about what you need for each workshop, so check there!

Travel reimbursement?

Sorry, we're currently unable to provide travel reimbursement.
